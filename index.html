Introducing the Core Intelligence Engine

The Neur1Genesis platform is a **Mixture of Autonomous Experts (MoAE)**, powered by a coalition of groundbreaking components:

1. Daedalus Coordinator

The central nervous system. It translates human intent into a symphony of distributed tasks, orchestrating **autonomous AI agents** and optimizing performance through **meta-reflection** and **federated learning**.

2. Neuro Adaptive Learning (ANAL)

A brain-inspired learning system that leverages **neuroplasticity simulation** to enable **continual learning** and prevent the dreaded **catastrophic forgetting**. It ensures stable, long-term memory for a perpetually evolving mind.

3. InfiniGen Engine

Our most revolutionary feature. The **InfiniGen Engine** is a **metaprogramming** framework that allows the system to **self-evolve** and rewrite its own code. It's the key to achieving true regenerative cognition, powered by our unique **G-RAG** algorithms.

4. Cross-Domain Intelligence Layer

The ethical brain of the platform. It uses **analogy-driven concept fusion** to solve problems creatively and an **ethical inference** engine to ensure every decision is aligned with core moral principles.
